desksprite

A tiny pixel office worker for your web page. Grab it, throw it, watch it eat lunch.

Try it

🖱️ Drag it off the desk — it dangles from the cursor, scared of heights. Drop / throw it — it falls and walks the floor. Toss it back onto the screen — it glides into the seat. It also wanders on its own now and then, and eats lunch at noon.
